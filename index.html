Why this exists
"My licensed software crashed on a Saturday.
Support wasn't available.
So I built my own."

I'm an international student from India studying philosophy in Belgium, financially independent since 19. When my university's dyslexia reading software failed over the weekend, I didn't have the option to wait. I built ReadEasy in two days.

Then I thought about the kids back home — in India, in South Asia — where neurodivergence is still misunderstood, underdiagnosed, or dismissed entirely. Kids who will never get a Kurzweil license. Kids whose parents think it's "all in their head." Kids who just need something that works, right now, on the phone they already have.

ReadEasy is for them. No account. No cost. No diagnosis required. Just open it and read.

What it does

Everything your brain needs

No clutter. No paywall. Just the tools that actually help you read.

🧠
Bionic Reading
Bolds the first half of every word so your eyes anchor faster and your brain fills the rest automatically.
📏
Reading Ruler
A highlight band follows your cursor so you never lose your place on the page.
🔊
Read Aloud
Highlights each word as it's spoken. Adjustable speed. Click any single word to hear it instantly.
⚡
Speed Reader
One word at a time, at your pace. Uses the Optimal Recognition Point so your brain processes faster with less effort.
📚
Tap to Define
Click any word for an instant definition, phonetics, and example sentence. No switching tabs.
🔦
Focus Mode
Dims everything except the paragraph you're reading. For when the whole page feels overwhelming.
📄
PDF Upload
Drag and drop any PDF — a textbook, article, paper — and read it with all accessibility features active.
🎨
Colour Themes
7 background colours including cream, blue, yellow, and dark mode — because white is not always the most readable.
🔤
Dyslexia Fonts
Lexend, Atkinson Hyperlegible, OpenDyslexic, and Georgia — fonts designed for easier reading, not just aesthetics.
